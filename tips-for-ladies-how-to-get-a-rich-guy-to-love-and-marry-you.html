How to get a rich guy to love and marry you

August 2024 · 3 minute read

But!!! be sure you genuinely love the guy oh! Money can never ever buy happiness.

1. Buy him expensive gifts: Most of the people around these rich guys want to take from them. So, if you turn the table and become the giver, you will catch his attention. Mind you, do not get him low class stuff. First of all find out what he loves, and get him the best or get him affordable quality things that will leave memories of you in his mind.

2. Be a good listener: These rich guys are the ones that call the shots. Everybody comes to them for advice and all. Everyday, they long to be heard. They want to share their inner thoughts with someone. If you can be that someone, you are in. Let them know you are available whenever they want to talk. Please make the offer like a lady. No funny winks or suggestive smiles and body movements. Please! I am not saying that you should become his teacher or preacher. Just sit there, be quiet, listen and go.

6. Dress well: There is a culture among the rich. If you want to get in,you have to dress like them. Let it be part of you. Dressing any how will give you off as someone who is irresponsible. Cover up every skin and cleavage. Yes! dress like holy sister or Nun if you like, but look smart and captivating.

10. Do not show off you cocky intelligence: Let things flow by themselves. Do not show off. Let them under-estimate you. It is better to take them by surprise. They will love you for that.
