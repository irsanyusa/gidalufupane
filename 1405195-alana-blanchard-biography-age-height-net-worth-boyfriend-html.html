Are Alana Blanchard and Bethany Hamilton still friends?

They have always been best friends. They got acquainted at school. Bethany Hamilton and Alana Blanchard spent a lot of time surfing together. When they grew up, they both got involved in professional sports.

Bethany survived a tiger shark attack on 31 October 2003. She lost her left hand then. You may be surprised to learn, but her trauma and the incident did not prevent her from surfing.
