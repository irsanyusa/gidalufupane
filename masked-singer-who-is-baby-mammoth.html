The Masked Singer just introduced its most adorably cute costume ever! And of course, it’s on Team Cuddly. We’re loving all the teams this season, but the Baby Mammoth costume is particularly on point. She has a pretty good voice to match too!

But who could Baby Mammoth be? That performance and the clues all lead to one person. Let’s find out who that is!

loading="lazy"

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 7

Before the season, the first clue we got for Baby Mammoth was a pair of ruby red slippers. For Baby Mammoth’s clues, she’s doing a hair care clue-mercial. She asks if “being stuck in the Ice Age” leaves your hair feeling “dull, dry, and downright dreadful”? Each of those words pops up in a text bubble. She says, “that used to be me until I discovered that it takes two products to strengthen…my hair.”

Baby Mammoth introduces her “cutting edge” mammpoo and conditioner. We see Men in Black sitting at salon chairs, reading magazines and drinking coffee with wigs on. She needs her hair “to match the star [she is] on the inside.” The camera zooms into a silver medal with a “2” on it. Baby Mammoth later explaining, “although I’ve gotten lots of silver, I want to go for the gold!” She describes herself (and her hair) as “strong, unbreakable, and iconic.” There’s a match put on display, then a “CHEER” megaphone and pom-poms.

After her performance, Baby Mammoth said, “They told me that I could do anything I wanted out here except poop on the stage. So I’m going to take a very big win because although you can poop in the circus, you are not allowed in this show.” She later chimes in, “No one’s saying I’m fat, right? Cause I just lost 300 pounds.”

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Baby Mammoth

Ken lead the predictions, thinking Baby Mammoth could be Nancy Kerrigan, then Jenny throws out Tonya Harding as a guess. Nicole “does better,” thinking it could be Kathleen Turner. Robin predicted Suzanne Somers from the clues, but from the voice thought Rachael Ray.

On social, fans are loving the Rachael Ray guess by Robin and Jenny! Some are still confused about whether this is a male or female though, with a lot guessing it could be Leslie Jordan. Wanda Sykes, Kirstie Alley, and Rebel Wilson are other popular predictions.

Who is Baby Mammoth on The Masked Singer?

Taking all the guesses into consideration, we could narrow down our search to a few top options. But looking into the clues, one quickly rose to the top!

After our research, we think that Baby Mammoth has to be…

Kirstie Alley!

And we are so happy to see it! She is an icon and deserves the spotlight. Here’s how all the clues line up.

The “sneak peek” clue with the ruby red slippers could be a nod to a Jenny Craig commercial Kirstie did that had strong influences from The Wizard of Oz. In her clues as a whole, this isn’t the first time she’s done a commercial for shampoo! In the 90’s, Kirstie did Pantene Pro V commercials. The text bubbles that popped up around words she was using in her clues could be a nod to her role the movie Look Who’s Talking. Her clue about a “star” could be mentioning her star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

A big clue we got was the silver “2” medal. Kirstie has appeared on both Dancing With the Stars and Celebrity Big Brother, placing second on both. So it makes sense that she’s coming on The Masked Singer to try for the gold again! The cheerleading clues point to her breakout role in the TV show Cheers. Another mention of a former role is her saying “it takes two,” because she starred in the 1995 movie It Takes Two.

Lastly, she made a mention of a “circus” after her performance and questioned the judges calling her fat, then saying she “lost 300 pounds.” These are all clues that directly point to her prior weight loss journey, leading to her being the spokesperson for both Jenny Craig and Organic Liaison throughout the years. The “circus” is a reference to a comment she got slack for, when she claimed she wasn’t “circus fat” but just needed to lose 30 pounds.
