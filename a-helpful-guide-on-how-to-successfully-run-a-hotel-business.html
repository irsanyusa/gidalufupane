A helpful guide on how to successfully run a hotel business

May 2024 · 5 minute read

Use a property management system

One of the best ways to improve your hotel business is by using a property management system. This type of software has been used in the hospitality industry for more than 30 years and it is still being used today because of its benefits.

The major benefit of this system is that it helps you automate some tasks such as sending emails, tracking data on customers and room information, and so on. That said, there are a couple of things that you should know about PMS for hotels and property management systems in general. First, they're not cheap—most cost hundreds or thousands per month to use.

ADVERTISEMENT

Second, they don't always play well with other types of software—operating rooms with third-party POS systems can sometimes be difficult. Third, these systems are designed to be more flexible than dedicated PMSs, so you'll need some time before you can master the tools.

You should also know that there are other great management systems out there besides PMSs—like RMS (resort management software), POS (point of sale) systems, and others. Again, just consider how much time it will take you to learn new features on a different piece of software. If it's not worth your time or money, stick with what you know.

Improve your website

One of the main reasons why hotel business owners should focus on improving their website is to ensure that they are providing relevant information based on what their customers are searching for.

For example, if you have a four or five-star hotel in New York City, it would be helpful to list all the amenities included in every room type so potential clients know exactly what they're getting. This makes it possible for them to find out everything they need without having to walk through your lobby.

Adjust your prices

One of the first steps you should take when beginning to run a motel is to adjust your prices so they meet certain standards. you have got to determine what kind of customers are coming through your doors, if it's mostly families or businessmen, then adjust accordingly so they have cheaper rates during the weekdays and more expensive rates on weekends. If it's the opposite way around then don't make these changes, keep everything fair and consistent throughout the year.

The other important thing to think about is how much money you should charge for your rooms. This is dependent on the amenities offered in each room, but also your location. don't price yourself out of business by charging extortionate prices or else people will go elsewhere! Remember that!!!

Make sure clients have access to their reservations 24/7

Besides being able to provide guests with frequent traveler loyalty programs, hotels should also put into place a 24/7 reservation system for people who would like information on a room or need help finding hotel services. This makes it convenient for them and gives them the opportunity to find exactly what they are looking for without wasting any time.

ADVERTISEMENT

Do not use printed brochures

One of the worst things you can do while looking for ways to improve your hotel business is using printed brochures. They are expensive, old school, and definitely need to be replaced by an online strategy. One of the main reasons why it is important that hotels move away from print advertising is that they're not effective in capturing new customers anymore.

The more technical your guests get with mobile devices and operating systems (Android or iOS), the more difficult it becomes for them to understand what you did before they walked into your hotel: printed brochures. In this case, simply offering them a tablet in the lobby, or downloading your app might do the trick.

Inform guests of all available amenities

Offering your guests a great experience from the moment they walk into your hotel is a key to success. And when it comes to improving their overall satisfaction, you should make sure that they know about all the available amenities and services in advance.

ADVERTISEMENT

That said, make sure you have a detailed list of everything that is included in the room rate. This would include Wi-Fi access, parking options, and any other add-ons that come with the cost of booking a room.

Make customer service part of your culture

Be aware that providing top-notch guest services is more than just offering your guests top-notch accommodations. This includes getting back to them in a timely manner, following up when they ask you for help, and honoring any promises that you make.

Customer service shouldn't be considered as an expense but rather as an investment. There are many ways to improve this aspect of your hotel business, including sending out surveys; collecting feedback; training employees; and taking advantage of tools that will measure how well employees perform on the job (by logging their interactions with customers).

Collect feedback after each guest's stay

The best way to measure customer satisfaction is to collect feedback after each guest's stay. This information is usually collected on a survey and includes the guest's room grade, service rating, and overall satisfaction with their stay. Gather all the positive inputs from customers as well as the negative ones. Use the data collected to further improve your operations and services.

Improve rates during off-peak hours

One of the best things about implementing dynamic pricing is that it can change prices depending on specific situations. For example, hotels could send out promotional offers based on the number of available rooms, day of the week, or season, ensuring that guests pay a lower rate during off-peak hours.

The key to a successful hotel business operation is the constant influx of customers throughout the year, not the sporadic or seasonal increase in guests.

There are many other things that may be taken into consideration when you're trying to run a successful hotel business. Just remember these few things before starting your own place and you should have no problems! Good luck!!!
