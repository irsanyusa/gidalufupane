&quot;Why You Should Not Get Married at 23&quot;: Evangelist Advises Ladies on Right Age to Marry, Gives Reaso

July 2024 · 4 minute read
  • An evangelist has given reasons why a young lady shouldn't get married at 23
  • According to him, many ladies do not have a clear view of their path in life and will come off as simple to any man they marry at that age
  • He went on to give an age he feels is ideal for women to get married and extended marriage advice to that male folks

An evangelist, Steven Ricky, has appealed to ladies to stop getting married in their early 20s.

Evangelist Steven said at 23, many ladies have yet to gain the wisdom to read men, analyse their character or even find their path in life.

He said marrying at 23 is a big risk for ladies as they make themselves pawns in the hands of their husbands.

On the most suitable marriage age, he told ladies to wait till their late 20s.
