10+ Times Celebritys Shoes Were Screaming For Attention

August 2024 · 4 minute read

During Cinderella’s momentous journey to the ball, her fairy godmother crafted a magnificent dress that left everyone in awe. However, the true essence of the fairy tale lay not in the attire but in the remarkable shoes. In reality, celebrities often don extraordinary footwear that effortlessly steals the spotlight, eclipsing their clothing and becoming the ultimate focal point.

Zendaya and the sparkling spiderweb

Invision / Invision / East News

Zendaya was literally sparkling at the Spider-Man: No Way Home premiere. She was wearing her Arc boots that were perfectly in line with the theme of the film. The heel “hanging” in the air looked very unusual.

Jennifer Lopez and her golden Christian Louboutin

Jennifer Lopez consistently manages to captivate the spotlight with her fashion choices. Back in 2012, she donned a pair of dazzling golden shoes from Christian Louboutin as she attended her single release event for “Goin In.”

The sparkling shoes truly complemented her overall appearance.

Kate Middleton and her golden splendor

At the premiere of the new James Bond film, Kate Middleton wore an amazing dress. And her golden Aquazzura shoes were the main focus of attention on the red carpet. We’re sure everyone was looking at the Duchess that night.

Jim Carrey and his biggest feet

AFP / East News

The renowned funnyman, Jim Carrey, made a bold fashion statement at Elton John’s AIDS Foundation Academy Awards Viewing Party back in 2013, sporting oversized prosthetic feet along with delicate angel wings. The actor said that the outfit was an expression of his ’awkward spiritual journey.’

Max DeAngelo, PacificCoastNews.com / EAST NEWS

He once again transformed into Bigfoot on The Late Show with David Letterman in New York the following month. Carrey didn’t shy away from his fans’ inquisitive gazes as he arrived at the studio. In fact, he graciously paused outside the venue, seizing the opportunity to strike poses for photographs and kindly sign autographs, showcasing his ever-charismatic persona.

Florence Pugh and her feather shoes

Another remarkable moment unfolded when Florence Pugh made a striking entrance, adorned in a complete ensemble from Valentino’s Fall 2022 collection, aptly named “The Beginning.” Her unforgettable appearance took place at The Venice Film Festival, where she graced the world premiere of Olivia Wilde’s Don’t Worry Darling.

To enhance her footwear, she opted for a dramatic boost by selecting bow-tied ankle straps adorned with cascades of elegant black feathers. This addition further amplified the overall glamour of Pugh’s ensemble, resulting in a head-to-toe statement.

Lady Gaga and her 24-inch platform shoes

Gregorio T. Binuya / Everett Collection / East News

Lady Gaga chose an amazing look for the CFDA awards. She wore a corset and train by Thierry Mugler, and 24-inch shoes. It was hard to walk in these shoes, so she had assistants to help her.

Rachel Brosnahan and cute petals

Quoin Pics / Everett Collection / East New

It’s hard to stop looking at the photos from The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel premier. Rachel Brosnahan, who plays the main character, was wearing amazing shoes that matched her suit. And the petals were such a nice detail!

Zendaya in a sea of pink

Cyril Pecquenard / KCS / East News

Zendaya was on the list of the most stylish stars that showed up at Paris Fashion Week for F/W 2022. She wore a monochromatic fuchsia suit with pink platform shoes.

Lil’ Kim and her shiny “bracelets”

Invision / Invision / East News

Singer Lil’ Kim visited the BET Awards wearing a black bodice dripping with jewels. Just look at the golden shoes with the “bracelets” that go all the way up to her hips. If you’re wondering how the entire construction doesn’t fall off her legs, there’s a strap on the back that keeps it all together!

Aurora James and the “globe”

Invision / Invision / East News

Designer Aurora James is on the list of the 100 most influential people in the world, according to Time. In the photo, she’s wearing shoes from her own brand, Brother Vellies. By the way, the decorative element between the big toe and the second toe is called a “globe.”

Kate Middleton and her 4-leaf clover

Usually, Kate Middleton wears classic shoes, but not this time. When she was visiting the Bahamas, she wore informal espadrilles with laces on the ankles.

We even noticed a cute detail: it looks like the ends of the laces have 4-leaf clovers on them, which, according to superstitions, brings good luck.

Bonus: Alexander McQueen is famous for his “Armadillo” shoes. They can be found at the Metropolitan Museum of Art

These shoes look like sea creatures for a reason: as inspiration, the designer imagined that due to an environmental disaster, humanity would start living underwater and mutate.

As the world marvels at the eccentric footwear choices of celebrities, one can’t help but wonder: What mind-boggling shoe styles will grace the red carpets next? From towering heels to futuristic designs, these celebrities have proven that shoes can speak louder than words.
