Career

After moving to Los Angeles, Rathbone began pursuing his acting career. He was cast in Disney 411, where he interviewed up and coming personalities.

He also had a guest role on The O.C and Close to Home. In 2005, he got his first role in the ABC Family original series Beautiful People, where he played the role of Nicholas Fiske.

In 2008, he played the role of Jasper Hale in Twilight. He reprised his role in the sequels to Twilight, The Twilight Saga: New Moon, The Twilight Saga: Eclipse and The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn part 1 and 2.

Charity work

Beside his singing and acting career, Rathbone has stretched an arm in helping the less fortunate in the society. He is a board member for the Little Kids Rock organization.
