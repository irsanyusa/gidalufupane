Why did Maia Mitchell leave Good Trouble?

After three seasons of portraying the character of Callie Adams Foster on Good Trouble, the actress left the show. When it was disclosed she would be leaving, fans wondered why.

She left the show because she felt the impact of being away from her loved ones, especially during the Covid-9 pandemic. She relocated to Australia from the United States of America, where the show was being filmed.
